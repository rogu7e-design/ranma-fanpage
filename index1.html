Welcome to the Ultimate Ranma ½ Fan Hub

Ranma ½ is a beloved martial arts romantic comedy series created by Rumiko Takahashi. This fan hub is dedicated to exploring everything about the series — from the original anime and manga, to OVAs and special episodes, character profiles, and fan events. Our goal is to provide a clean, organized space for fans to enjoy information, stay updated with news, and explore the world of Ranma ½.

Main Characters

Ranma Saotome

Martial arts prodigy cursed to turn into a girl when splashed with cold water. The central character navigating romance and rivalry.

Akane Tendo

Strong-willed, caring, and Ranma’s childhood friend. Her evolving relationship with Ranma drives much of the story’s charm.
